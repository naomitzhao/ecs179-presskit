About

Bike Theft Simulator's core gameplay system involves an open map with a main objective of stealing bikes from the UC Davis campus.

Play across multiple levels in a 2D top-down world, avoid being spotted by NPCs, and make money from becoming the best UC Davis bike thief!

Bike Theft Simulator is free to play on our web release.

Features

  • Shiny (or not so shiny) bikes to steal for profit!
  • NPCs to avoid so you don't get caught...
  • A suspicious looking alleyway shop to buy all of your bike-theft essentials
  • College campus map to explore and steal from!
